    Home

    Greetings! I am a 4th-year BSc student in the ECE department at the University of Tehran. Currently, I am actively engaged in research supervised by Dr. Mo Lotfollahi. My research focuses on developing a multi-modal feature extractor model that utilizes a BERT-like transformer architecture and contrastive learning approach. My interests lie in the field of deep learning, with a particular focus on explainability, fairness, and interpretability of deep models. As an aspiring researcher, I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills.

    In addition to my primary research, my interests also include deep generative models, computational biology, self-supervised, and multi-modal learning. Currently, I am working on addressing spurious correlations in models trained using ERM.
