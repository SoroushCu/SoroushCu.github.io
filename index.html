    Home

    Hello! I'm a senior 4th-year student pursuing a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering, specializing in Communications, at the University of Tehran's ECE department. Alongside my studies, I serve as a Research and Teaching Assistant within the Communications Branch. I'm currently engaged in research in the Signal Processing and Communication Systems Laboratory under the guidance of Prof. Ali Olfat. Currently, my main research thrust involves optimization within wireless and massive MIMO systems. Notably, I recently completed my B.Sc. thesis, which focused on designing an algorithm using iterative algorithms that harness sparse properties. As a dedicated researcher and student, I'm continually in search of fresh challenges and opportunities to broaden my knowledge and enhance my skills.

    Beyond my primary research, my interests extend into various areas, including Communication Theory, Statistical Signal Processing, Estimation Theory. I'm also genuinely intrigued by the practical applications of machine learning and reinforcement learning models within communication systems. Furthermore, I'm keen on bridging classical communication principles with cutting-edge technologies.
